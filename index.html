A Message From Susan Shelton Perry


Dear Friends,

I have been practicing law now for 39 years. I hope you feel that you can rely on my perspective on this issue. An Intermediate Appellate Court would be a total waste of money for WV.

If YOU get injured and the jury rules in your favor, an Intermediate Appellate Court would let the other party appeal this decision TWO times. Even if you are represented by a “no fee if no recovery” lawyer, the fees will be much larger when you do eventually get your award.

You will get less and lawyers will get more. The Intermediate Appellate Court is a tactic used to discourage people from filing suits, or if they file, to convince Plaintiffs to settle for smaller amounts. Don’t fall for this.
